Taj Mahal

Located in Agra, India, the Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Eiffel Tower

Standing tall in Paris, France, the Eiffel Tower was designed by engineer Gustave Eiffel. It was constructed for the 1889 Exposition Universelle and has since become one of the most recognizable structures in the world.

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is an ancient series of walls and fortifications that stretch over 13,000 miles. It was built to protect China from invasions, and it's one of the longest man-made structures in the world.

Colosseum

Located in the heart of Rome, Italy, the Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater that hosted gladiatorial games and public spectacles. It is a symbol of the Roman Empire's architectural brilliance.

Christ the Redeemer

The Christ the Redeemer statue stands atop Mount Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. This iconic statue of Jesus Christ is one of the largest statues of its kind and offers stunning views of the city.
